Hello! I am a computational linguist interested in how language, culture, and storytelling help people make sense of the world.

I graduated summa cum laude with a B.S. in Computer Science (minors in Linguistics, Mathematics, and Scandinavian Studies) from Brigham Young University. I am currently pursuing an M.A. in Linguistic Diversity and Digital Humanities at the University of Helsinki.

My work bridges the gap between computer science and human linguistics. I focus on developing machine learning applications for low-resource languages, designing systems for multilingual human-computer interaction, and studying how translation and digital storytelling can preserve linguistic diversity and promote cross-cultural compassion.
